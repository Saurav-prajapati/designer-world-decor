Our Services

At Designer World Decor, we offer a wide range of high-quality interior decor solutions to transform your space with style and functionality. Our expertise ensures durable, elegant, and modern designs tailored to your needs.

Wallpaper Installation (2D/3D)

Enhance your walls with stunning 2D and 3D wallpapers that add depth and character to any space. Choose from a variety of designs, textures, and colors to complement your interiors.

PVC & WPC Panel Work

Upgrade your interiors with PVC and WPC panels, which offer durability, water resistance, and a sleek, modern look. These panels are ideal for walls and ceilings, providing a stylish yet low-maintenance finish.

WPC & PVC Board Installation

Perfect for furniture, partitions, and wall cladding, our WPC and PVC boards are strong, eco-friendly, and resistant to moisture and termites. They provide a long-lasting and aesthetically pleasing solution for any setting.

Flooring & Vinyl Solutions

Give your floors a premium finish with our vinyl and wooden flooring solutions. We offer stylish, durable, and easy-to-maintain flooring options that enhance the look and feel of your space.

ACP (Aluminum Composite Panel) Work

Our ACP work is perfect for both interior and exterior applications, providing a sleek and modern aesthetic. These panels are lightweight, weather-resistant, and highly durable.

False Ceiling Work

Enhance the look of your home or office with our expertly crafted false ceilings. From POP to gypsum ceilings, we create unique designs that improve lighting, insulation, and overall ambiance.

Aluminum & Steel Fabrication

We specialize in aluminum and steel work, providing strong and stylish solutions for doors, windows, partitions, and more. Our metal fabrication services ensure durability and elegance in every project.

Electrical & Lighting Solutions

Lighting plays a crucial role in interior design. Our team provides professional electrical and lighting solutions, from modern LED setups to ambient lighting, to create the perfect atmosphere for your space.

Modular Kitchen & Bathroom Work

Upgrade your kitchen and bathroom with our modular designs, offering functionality, storage efficiency, and a sleek appearance. We provide customized solutions tailored to your space and lifestyle.

Paint & Texture Work (2D/3D)

Give your walls a refreshing makeover with our paint and texture work. We offer a variety of textures, finishes, and 3D effects to add depth and elegance to your interiors.
